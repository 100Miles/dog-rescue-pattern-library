Why rescue a dog?

In short, it saves at least one life. In long, there are overcrowding problems in shelters across the country and pets deserving of warm, loving homes are scheduled for euthenasia. Also, when you adopt a pet it frees up room and resources for the animal that will take its place. And this says nothing of the love, affection and enrichment that will be provided to you!
